# Biography[English]
Much of my research is driven by the statistical analysis of data arising in genomics and biomedicine.   My students, collaborators, and I are working on a variety of problems that involve statistical applications, methodology, and theory.  Descriptions of some of these problems, and of some more theoretically oriented work, can be found at the links below.
